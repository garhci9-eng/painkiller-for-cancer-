## Scientific Background / 과학적 근거

Target selection based on published research in non-opioid analgesia:  
비마약성 진통 타겟 선정은 다음 연구에 근거합니다:

- **Nav1.7/Nav1.8**: Cox et al. (2006). *An SCN9A channelopathy causes congenital inability to experience pain.* Nature.
- **TRPV1**: Caterina et al. (1997). *The capsaicin receptor: a heat-activated ion channel in the pain pathway.* Nature.
- **P2X3**: Ford et al. (2006). *P2X3 and P2X2/3 receptor antagonist development.* Current Opinion in Drug Discovery & Development.
- **NK1**: Hill (2000). *NK1 (substance P) receptor antagonists.* Current Pharmaceutical Design.
